Vintage Mud Cloth Throw - Bambara Style

CFA 45,000 CFA 52,000

This authentic Bogolanfini is hand-dyed using fermented mud and plant leaves, a tradition passed down through generations in the Bamana culture. The rich, earthy tones are achieved without chemicals, harnessing the power of the Niger River's clay.

This specific pattern represents the crocodile, a symbol of protection in Bambara culture. Each cloth takes approximately two weeks to complete, drying under the intense Sahelian sun to lock in the colors.
